Our Expertise

Comprehensive Mould Categories

We manufacture a wide range of moulds tailored to your specific production needs.

Injection Mould

High-precision moulds for mass production of complex plastic parts with tight tolerances.

Blow Mould

Specialized tooling for manufacturing hollow plastic parts such as bottles and containers.

Die Casting Mould

Robust moulds designed for forcing molten metal under high pressure into mold cavities.

Silicone / Rubber Mould

Flexible and durable moulds for producing high-quality rubber and silicone components.

Thermoforming Mould

Also known as Vacuum Moulds, ideal for packaging trays, blisters, and large plastic shells.

CNC & Lathe Works

Expert CNC machining and lathe work for custom tooling parts, repairs, and modifications.
